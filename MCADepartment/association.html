The Department has MCA (Masters, Creators,Achievers) Association through which the Dept conducts activities like Technical seminars, Group Discussion, Technical Aptitude and Programming test to update and help the students for the better placements.

Students are encouraged to participate in various activities conducted by association. These activities aid students in their preparation for recruitment thrugh campus or outside campus.

Student Association Activities

  • Freshers FEST was organized on 29th October 2011.
  • One Day student workshop on OOAD using UML was organized on 9th august 2011. About 28 students participated. Experts from Cranes Software(IBM Certified Professionals) took the sessions on OOAD, UML.
  • Four Days faculty workshop conducted on OOAD using UML and Rational Software Architecture.
  • Students association of MCA organized an inter Department IT and Cultural Fest "Sample-2K12" on 20th April 2012 and conducted various activities such as coding Debugging, IT Quiz, Web designing, Mad Ads, Dumb Charades, Rangolli etc.
  • Farewell party for the outgoing Students of MCA organized on 21st April 2012.
  • Students Association conducted various activities like SELF-INTRODUCTION, ACT TO SITUATION AND COME WITH THE SOLUTION, and GROUP DISCUSSION etc.
