Vision

To be among the best of the institution for engineers and technologist with attitude, Skills and knowledge and to become an epicenter of creative solutions

Mission

To achieve and impart quality education with an emphasis on practical skills an social relevance

MCA is a three-year Post Graduation program having six semesters. The syllabus of first semester is framed with the subjects that provide a base for the rest of the semesters in an effective way to impart skills and knowledge about the subjects. In second and third semesters, core Computer science subjects are introduced with programming practical labs. In the fourth and fifth semesters, students can select two elective papers out of five in each semester along with five core subjects and two practical labs. Sixth semester comprises of project work in the area of students interest on any application development as required by any internship supported organization while implementing the needs of the clients.


Available Opportunities

Software Developer,Software Engineer,Team Leader,Project Leader,Project Manager


Eligibility

Students who have completed BCA or B.Sc. Computer Science/Maths/Statistics/Physics.


Duration

MCA degree shall extend over a period of three academic years with six semesters.
