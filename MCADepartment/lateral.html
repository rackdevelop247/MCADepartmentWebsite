MCA Lateral is a 2 year Post Graducation program Affiliated to Random,Sikar and recognized by AICTE, New Delhi.Students will be directly taking admission to 3rd Semester of 2 Year MCA program.In 3rd semester,core Computer science subjects are introduced with programming practical labs. In the fourth and fifth semesters, students can select two elective papers out of five in each semester along with five core subjects and two practical labs. Sixth semester comprises of project work in the area of students interest on any application development as required by any internship supported organization while implementing the needs of the clients.


Available Opportunities

Software Developer,Software Engineer,Team Leader,Project Leader,Project Manager


Eligibility

Students who have completed BCA or B.Sc. Computer Science/Maths/Statistics/Physics.


Duration

MCA degree shall extend over a period of three academic years with six semesters.
