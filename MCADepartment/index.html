About us

Master of computer applications program had its inception in the academic year 2008-09; to meet growing demand of industries for personnel specialized in the fields of computers and its application and to enable graduates from science, commerce and BCA to enter into mainstream IT industry. The department has made a respectable recognition among the institutes offering similar course. The specialty of this department is that all the faculty members are master degree holder from reputed institutes with many years of teaching experience.Faculty members dynamically upload all their lecture and question banks on the EWIT Website for students. The department regularly arranges for Guest lecture on topics in cutting edge technologies.The focus areas of the department are-

  • Database Management Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • System Software
  • Web Technologies
  • Networking

The department has 6 lecture halls and 6 full fledged laboratories to conduct all the experiments prescribed by the university. Laboratory has 120 high-end systems with latest configuration and connected to LAN.

The Program

The curriculum of the program has been designed to cater to the ever changing needs and demands of IT industry. The focus is on holistic learning and empowering students to make significant contribution at all levels. The Master of computer application curriculum spreads over 6 semesters which includes courses for 5 semesters aimed at transmitting the broad spectrum of knowledge and 6th semester industrial training with intern ships in reputed organizations.
